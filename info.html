DRAKE

Born: October 24, 1986 (age 32 years), Toronto, Canada

Citizenship: Canada; United States

Net worth: US$140 million (2019)

Aubrey Drake Graham is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, actor, and entrepreneur. As an entrepreneur, Drake has founded the OVO Sound record label with longtime collaborator 40.
